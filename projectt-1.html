How It Works

Download the App

Students download the SSA Smart ID app from the college website.

Create Account

Students and guards create their accounts to access the system.

Generate QR Code

Students receive a unique QR code for verification.

Scan & Verify

Guards scan the QR code to verify student status and access data.
