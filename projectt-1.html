SSA Smart ID

Revolutionizing Student Identity and Attendance Management

What is SSA Smart ID?

SSA Smart ID is an innovative Android application designed to streamline student identity verification and attendance management. It provides a seamless experience for students, security guards, and teachers.

How It Works

Step 1: Student Admission

After completing the admission process, students download the SSA app from the college website.

Step 2: Create Account

Students fill in their details and create an account on the app.

Step 3: Generate QR Code

Students generate a unique QR code containing their academic information.

Step 4: Security Verification

Security guards scan the QR code to verify the student's identity and college affiliation.

Step 5: Attendance Tracking

Teachers access real-time attendance records through the app.

Key Features

Geolocation Restriction

The app works only within the college's geolocation for enhanced security.

Real-Time Updates

Instant updates on attendance and student records.

Secure Data

All student data is encrypted and securely stored.
